Communication

Master the art of expressing ideas clearly, confidently, and effectively in any situation.

Overview

Communication is a key soft skill involving verbal, non-verbal, and written expression. It’s essential for collaboration, leadership, and professional success.

What You’ll Be Able to Do

  • Communicate clearly in teams and meetings
  • Write professional emails and documents
  • Deliver presentations confidently
  • Use active listening and empathy during conversations

Recommended Learning Path

  1. Basics of Verbal & Non-Verbal Communication
  2. Public Speaking and Presentation Skills
  3. Email & Written Communication
  4. Listening and Feedback Techniques
  5. Conflict Resolution through Communication

Challenges

  • Daily: Practice active listening in conversations
  • Weekly: Record a 2-minute presentation on any topic
