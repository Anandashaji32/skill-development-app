Entrepreneurship

Learn how to build, launch, and grow your own business successfully.

Overview

Entrepreneurship is the process of identifying a business opportunity, developing a product or service, and launching a startup to meet market needs.

What You’ll Be Able to Do

  • Generate and evaluate business ideas
  • Create a solid business plan
  • Understand funding, marketing, and scaling
  • Launch and manage your own startup
